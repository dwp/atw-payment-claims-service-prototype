What you will need to make a claim

You will need to tell us:

  • dates that you travelled to or from work
  • the details of the person or company we need to pay - this could be you, a family member or friend, your employer or a transport company

If you want us to pay a person or company we have not paid before, you'll need to give their name, address, email and bank details.

If you are an employee

You will need the name and email address of a workplace contact we can ask to confirm each journey you are claiming for. This needs to be someone like your line manager or HR manager.
