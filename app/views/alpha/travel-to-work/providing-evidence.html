How to send us copies of receipts or invoices

We accept:

  • photos or scans of paper receipts or invoices
  • digital receipts or invoices

Your receipts or invoices must show the:

  • cost of the journey
  • date of the journey
  • full name of the person or company who provided transport

You can either send:

  • a receipt or invoice for all the journeys you are claiming for
  • separate receipts and invoices for journeys - up to a limit of 90 uploaded files for each claim
