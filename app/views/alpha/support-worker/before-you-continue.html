What you will need to make a claim

You will need to tell us:

  • the dates you had support
  • how many hours of support you had on each date
  • the details of the person or company we need to pay - this can be you, a family member, friend, employer or anyone who paid for your support worker costs
  • the name, address, email and bank details of any new person or company we haven't paid before

You will need to send a separate claim for each type of support you get. For example, if you use a British Sign Language interpreter and have a driver, you will need to send 2 claims.

If different people or companies paid for your support, you'll also need to send separate claims for each.

How to confirm your claim

You will need to give us:

  • the name and email address of the person or company we can ask to confirm the support
  • an invoice or receipt for any support worker costs you claim for

If you are self-employed or work at the same organisation as the person providing support, you will need to send us a digital copy of a signed timesheet or payslip for the person who provided your support.
