What you’ll need to make a claim

You can claim for more than one piece of specialist equipment.

Information you'll need to continue

You'll need to tell us:

  • the date you bought the equipment – you can find this on the receipt or invoice
  • details of the person or company we need to pay - this can be you, a family member, friend, employer or anyone who paid for your equipment

If you want us to pay a person or company we have not paid before, you'll need to give their name, address, email and bank details.

Evidence you'll need to provide

You'll need to send us an invoice or receipt for the equipment you are claiming for.
