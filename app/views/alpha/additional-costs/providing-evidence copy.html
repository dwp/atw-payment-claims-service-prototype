How to send us copies of receipts or invoices

We accept either:

  • photos or scans of paper receipts or invoices
  • digital receipts or invoices

Your receipts or invoices must show:

  • the amount paid for the support worker
  • the support worker's full name
  • the dates the support worker provided support
  • a short description of the support they provided

Taking photos or scans of receipts or invoices

Make sure:

  • you include the whole receipt or invoice
  • the photo or scan is not blurry
  • you take photos from above and not at an angle
  • you take photos in a well-lit place
